Headroom Mini

Your Claude usage, on your desk. Let's get it running.

⚙️ Setup runs best in Chrome or Edge on a computer (the browser flasher needs USB access). Phones and Safari/Firefox can't flash — but you'll only do this once.
1

Flash the board

Plug the board into your computer with a data USB-C cable (a charge-only cable won't work), then:

This browser can't flash — open this page in Chrome or Edge on a computer. Not allowed over http — use the https link.

Pick the board's port when asked, then Install. Takes about a minute.

Board not showing up?

Hold the BOOT button, tap RESET, release BOOT, then click Connect again. If there's still no port, try a different USB-C cable — most are charge-only.

2

Put it on your Wi-Fi

When it reboots, the screen shows a setup network. On your phone:

  1. Join the Wi-Fi named Headroom-Setup (password headroom).
  2. A page opens automatically (or visit http://192.168.4.1).
  3. Tap your home network from the list and enter its password.

The board reboots and shows its address on screen — jot it down.

3

Feed it your usage

Run the little companion on the computer where you use Claude Code. It reads your existing login and sends usage to the board — no extra sign-in. Download it (no install, just double-click):

Windows.exe macOSApple / Intel Linuxx86-64

It finds the board on your network automatically. Within a minute or two the meters go live.

It didn't find the board?

Point it straight at the address from step 2 by launching it from a terminal with --pi http://<board-ip>:8080. Keep the companion running (or set it to start with your computer) so the meters stay fresh.

That's it — no VS Code, no command line.
